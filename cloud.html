Cloud Engineer at
Amazon

Jan 2020 - Jan 2021

As a Cloud Engineer at Amazon, I immersed myself in the vast ecosystem of AWS, contributing to the management and optimization of cloud infrastructure. My role involved a mix of automation, deployment, and direct collaboration with various engineering teams to enhance our cloud services.

Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

  • Enhanced operational efficiency by over 90% through centralized management of multiple tasks and scheduling of enterprise-wide tasks.
  • Utilized Python, Terraform, Chef, and Ansible for scripting, configuration management, and implementing Infrastructure as Code.
  • Managed the development of a robust Cloud platform on AWS architecture, ensuring high availability and scalability.
  • Collaborated with 3 engineering teams for AWS Role Management and resource provisioning.
  • Conducted cloud deployments across IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS platforms, integrating data center services with cloud solutions.
  • Managed 100% of existing AWS Cloud environments, including automation, monitoring, disaster recovery, and capacity planning.
  • Proficiently used Ansible, Terraform, and ARM Templates for configuration management and scripted Infrastructure as Code with PowerShell, JSON, YAML, and Python.

Working at Amazon provided me with invaluable experience in managing large-scale cloud environments and deepened my expertise in cloud technologies and automation. It was a transformative experience that reinforced my passion for cloud engineering and its potential to revolutionize business operations.
