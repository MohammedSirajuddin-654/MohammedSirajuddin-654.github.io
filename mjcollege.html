Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Instrumentation
Engineering at
Muffakham Jah College of Engineering & Technology

Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology -- Hyderabad, TS, 2014 - 2018
 Ranked in the top 3 Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering college
 Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Instrumentation Completed Courses in Major:
Automation in process control, Process instrumentation, Virtual Instrumentation, Advanced PLC Programming, Power Plant design and Safety Management, Digital signal Processing, Image processing, Industrial administration and Financial Management.

The four year B.E. program in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering was started in the year 1997 in order to meet the surging industry demand for professionals in the area of automation and Power who could monitor and control automated engineering systems. The laboratories in the department have the state-of-the-art equipment. The department also has a computer center for laboratory courses like Micro processors and micro controllers, digital signal processing, simulation studies and also for the use of senior students in their project work and seminars.
